Hogwarts Legacy

Hogwarts Legacy is an immersive, open-world action RPG set in the world first introduced in the Harry Potter books.
Here, you can find the latest news about the game, deep-dives guides, videos and more.
Hogwarts Legacy is currently available on PlayStation®5, PlayStation®4, PlayStation®4 Pro, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One consoles and PC.
