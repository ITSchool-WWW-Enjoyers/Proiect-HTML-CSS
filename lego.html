Check out the new LEGO Harry Potter Gringotts Wizarding Bank set

This latest LEGO® set invites you to build Diagon Alley's iconic wizarding bank Gringotts, complete with 13 minifigures and the Ukranian Ironbelly Dragon.

Gringotts is known as one of the most intricate and cleverly designed
locations across all of the wizarding world, comprised of underground
tunnels and secret vaults. Now, you can celebrate the dazzling details of the
bank with this brand new upcoming LEGO® set, which uses 4,803
pieces! (So be careful when you're hoovering!)

Fans will be able to build the grandiose white exterior of Gringotts, the main
banking hall, and of course, the best bit: the mysterious underground vaults,
which come with a mine-cart rollercoaster ride. You may remember
Hagrid and Harry once took the plunge on this cart in the very first
Harry Potter film.

The Gringotts set also encompasses some of the adventures from the later
Harry Potter stories, such as the epic break-in (and break-out!) that Harry, Ron
and Hermione (all included as minifigures!) achieve during Harry Potter and
the Deathly Hallows. This means that you'll get your very own Ukranian
Ironbelly Dragon as part of this set, who is freed by
the golden trio.

Other minifigures include Rubeus Hagrid, Bellatrix Lestrange, Griphook and
various goblin bankers and guards, meaning you can build your very own
Gringotts andhave your own adventures. It's definitely more exciting than our
Muggle banks!

The LEGO® Harry Potter™ Gringotts Wizarding Bank™
set is available on September 4th, or 1st September for LEGO VIPs!
Learn more over at the official website. But it'll be able to pre-order from 21st
August!
